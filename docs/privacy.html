Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Diabeto ("the App") is a personal diabetes health tracking app developed by Naman Rastogi. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. By using Diabeto, you agree to this policy.

1. Data We Collect

The App collects the following health data, with your explicit permission:

  • Blood glucose readings — timestamps and mg/dL values from Apple HealthKit
  • Sleep data — sleep duration, stages (deep, REM, light), bedtime, and wake time from Apple HealthKit
  • Exercise and workout data — workout type, start time, and duration from Apple HealthKit
  • Account information — your email address, used to create and authenticate your account

We do not collect location data, contacts, browsing history, financial information, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Health insights — your health data is sent to the Anthropic Claude AI API to generate personalised insights about your glucose trends, sleep patterns, and exercise habits
  • Dashboard and charts — data is stored and displayed within the App so you can view your trends over time
  • Pattern detection — the App analyses correlations between glucose, sleep, and exercise to surface actionable observations

Your data is used solely to provide the App's features to you. We do not use your health data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to your personal health tracking.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your health data is stored in a private, encrypted database hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) RDS in the US West (Oregon) region
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • Access to your data requires authentication via AWS Cognito — only you can access your account's data
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely in your device's Keychain
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your health data with any third parties, except as described in Section 4

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic Claude API — your health data (glucose readings, sleep summaries, exercise records) is sent to Anthropic's API to generate AI-powered insights and respond to your chat questions. Data sent to Anthropic is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. Anthropic does not use your data to train its models by default.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — data storage (RDS), API hosting (API Gateway, Lambda), and authentication (Cognito). Subject to AWS's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple HealthKit — the App reads health data from HealthKit with your permission. The App does not write any data back to HealthKit.

5. HealthKit Data

Diabeto accesses Apple HealthKit data solely to provide health tracking features within the App. HealthKit data will not be used for advertising, sold to data brokers, or shared with third parties except as described in this policy (i.e., AI analysis via Anthropic). This is consistent with Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

6. Data Retention

Your health data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, all your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all your data is visible within the App
  • Delete your data — contact us to request full account and data deletion
  • Revoke HealthKit access — go to iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Diabeto
  • Export your data — available on request

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
